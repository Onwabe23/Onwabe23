About Me

Hello, I'm Onwabe Zibeke, a software developer who recently graduated from Durban University of Technology with a NQF level 7 degree in Information and Communication Technology. Throughout my academic journey, I specialized in Software Development, which equipped me with a solid foundation in coding, problem-solving, and project management.

I am passionate about crafting efficient and elegant solutions to real-world problems through code. My drive stems from a deep-seated curiosity and a love for technology. I thrive on challenges and constantly seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skill set.

My expertise spans various technologies and frameworks, including React, EJS, Node.js with Express, and MongoDB. I am proficient in front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, TailwindCSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. In my projects, I strive to write clean, maintainable code and implement best practices to ensure high-quality software.

While I do not have professional experience, I have gained substantial experience through hands-on projects. These projects have allowed me to apply my skills in real-world scenarios, collaborate with peers, and deliver functional and user-friendly applications.

As I embark on my professional journey, my goal is to contribute to innovative projects that make a meaningful impact. I am particularly interested in areas such as web development and mobile applications.
